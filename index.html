Our Story

Little Bites Bakery was founded in 2015 by Sarah Mensah, a passionate baker who turned her love for baking into a thriving business in Malejor-Accra.

What started as a small home-based operation has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery, known for its fresh, high-quality baked goods made with love and attention to detail.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, sourcing locally whenever possible, and baking everything fresh daily. Our mission is to bring joy to our community through delicious baked goods.
